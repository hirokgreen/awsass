Students:

The users who submit their project in system. They are able to access their account and see their tasks.
The user get the following advantages:
• Cost saving, eliminates the unnecessary meeting and the cost of travel.
• Time line of the project is visible thus deadlines can be achieved on time.
• Higher performance, students can manage their projects a lot easier with a decent project management time line and deadline.
• Convenient for students and supervisors, using the online student management system is a lot easier to use since they can simply go online 24/7 without any time restriction.
• Higher security and privacy.
